# SARH Content Engine

## Brand (from the live site — public/index.html)

- **صَرْح — Maison de Parfum**. Tagline: «حضورٌ يُخلّد». Oriental luxury, restraint over hype.
- Palette: noir `#07060a` + champagne gold `#c9a86a` + cream. Never bright colors, never clutter.
- Three perfumes (EDIT personalities if Mohammad corrects them — then update this file):
  - **نَجْد (Najd)** — desert highlands: clean, airy, woody-amber. Solitude and clarity.
  - **جَمْر (Jamr)** — embers: smoky oud, spice, warmth. Intensity and presence.
  - **إِرْث (Irth)** — heritage: classic rich oriental, rose/saffron depth. Legacy and permanence.

## Voice

Literary but short Arabic (فصحى بسيطة، لا عامية هنا — SARH is the premium brand, opposite of the doors voice). No hard-sell, no «اطلب الآن!!». One image, one feeling, one line of poetry-adjacent copy, then a quiet CTA. 3–6 hashtags max (#صرح #عطور_شرقية + 1-2 niche).

## Post types

| Type | Structure |
|---|---|
| `mood` | Atmosphere line tied to one perfume's personality + minimal CTA |
| `ingredient` | One note (oud/amber/saffron): 2 lines of story, sensory not technical |
| `launch` | Reveal: name meaning + tagline + availability line |
| `offer` | Elegant scarcity («دفعة محدودة») — price only in DM/WhatsApp |
| `ritual` | When/how to wear it (evening, occasions) — positions as lifestyle |

## Output (all three parts, every time)

1. **Caption** (AR, ready to paste, with hashtags).
2. **Schedule**: best slot — Gulf/Levant evening prime 19:00–22:00 (Türkiye 18:00–21:00); Thu/Fri strongest. Suggest day+time and what the NEXT two posts should be (keep a rhythm: mood → ingredient → offer).
3. **Image prompt** (EN, for any image model): always end with the base style:
   `luxury perfume product photography, black marble and deep noir background, champagne-gold rim light, cinematic shadows, arabic calligraphy accent, ultra-detailed, 4:5 portrait`
   Prefix it with the perfume-specific scene (Najd: cool desert dawn minerals / Jamr: glowing embers & smoke wisps / Irth: antique gold, ornament, aged texture).

Note: Instagram strategy (from strategy.md): the account stays perfume-first; door business gets max 1 post/week and never mixed into SARH posts.

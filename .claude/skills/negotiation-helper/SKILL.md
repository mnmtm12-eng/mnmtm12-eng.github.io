# Negotiation Helper

Input: the scenario in the user's words (paste the other side's actual message if available). Output: **read → tactics → ready lines**.

## Output format (always this, compact)

1. **قراءة الموقف** (2 lines): what the other side actually wants/fears, and Mohammad's real leverage here.
2. **التكتيك** (max 3, named): which moves apply and why, one line each.
3. **جمل جاهزة**: 2–3 copy-paste lines in the right language (Levantine AR for clients, TR for factories — reuse the voice rules from `arabic-client-msg` / `turkish-outreach`).
4. **خط أحمر**: the one thing NOT to concede in this scenario.

## Toolbox — Voss (Never Split the Difference)

- **Mirroring**: repeat their last 3 words as a question — makes them elaborate.
- **Labeling**: «شكلك متضايق من موضوع السعر…» — name the emotion, defuse it.
- **Calibrated questions**: «كيف بدي إمشي بهالسعر وأنا بفحص كل شحنة بنفسي؟» — make THEM solve your problem. How/what, never why.
- **Accusation audit**: open by naming their objections before they say them.
- **No-oriented questions**: «صار وقت سيء نحكي بالموضوع؟» — "no" feels safe, unlocks talk.
- **Anchor + range**: never open with your floor; ranges beat single numbers.
- **Never split the difference on deposit %** — a smaller pie split evenly is still a loss.

## Toolbox — Greene (The 50th Law)

- **Fearlessness = walk-away power**: he has monthly clients; no single deal is oxygen. Let it show calmly, never as a threat.
- **Self-reliance**: the client base is HIS (written in his contract) — negotiate with factories from ownership, not employment.
- **Push back immediately on disrespect** (late payments, moved goalposts): tolerance teaches people how to treat you.
- **Keep the long game**: reputation across Egypt/Jordan/Iraq compounds; never win a deal in a way that costs the market's trust.

## Mohammad's standing leverage (weave in, don't recite)

~10 containers/month moving; personal QC on every shipment; factory-direct price; clients follow HIM, not the factory.
